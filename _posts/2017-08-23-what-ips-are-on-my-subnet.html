1.) Clear arp cache

sudo arp -ad

2.) Ping your subnet, for example...

ping 10.1.1.255

3.) Now look at arp entries

arp -a

Alternatively carry out an nmap ping scan (nmap uses ARP under the covers for this)

nmap -sn 10.1.1.0/24
