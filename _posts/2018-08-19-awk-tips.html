looping:

cat people.data | awk 'BEGIN{a=0} {printf("%d,%s\n", a++, $0);}'

 

printing path elements:

assuming the path /one/two/three/four/five/foo.jar

find /one/two -name foo.jar | awk -F/ '{print $(NF-1)}'

Will output:

five
